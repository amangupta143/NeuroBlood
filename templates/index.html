The Science Behind NeuroBlood

Dermatoglyphics & Genetic Correlation

Fingerprint patterns (dermatoglyphics) are formed during fetal development and are influenced by the same genetic factors that determine blood type. Our research has established strong correlations between specific ridge patterns and ABO/Rh blood groups.

These genetic connections allow our algorithms to identify subtle pattern variations that correspond to different blood types with remarkable accuracy.

Deep Learning Architecture

NeuroBlood utilizes a proprietary neural network architecture that combines convolutional neural networks (CNNs) with transformer modules to extract both local and global features from fingerprint images.

Our model has been trained on a diverse dataset of over 6,000 fingerprint samples across all blood types, ensuring robust performance across different demographics and image qualities.
